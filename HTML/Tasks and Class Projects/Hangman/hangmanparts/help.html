About Hangman

Hangman is a simple word game where the player attempts to discover a secret word by guessing one letter at a time. Each time the player guesses a letter which does not appear in the word, a part hanging man's picture is drawn. Further,you must discover the hiden word if needed by taking the given hint before the man is hanged.

To Win

The executioner i.e (here the computer) wins if the full body is hanging from the gallows. The guesser(player) wins if they guess the word before the person is hung.
